Frequently asked questions

How and when will I receive the sacred prasad and consecrated items?

After the ritual is performed, the priests sanctify the offerings. Dry fruits, sacred kumkum, energized threads, and prasad are packaged in hermetic food-grade pouches and dispatched via express courier with live tracking, reaching your doorstep within 3-5 business days.

What should I do with the prasad once it arrives?

Prasad is considered sacred and should be handled with respect. It's traditionally recommended to consume dry sweets or fruits on an empty stomach after bathing, and to tie sacred red threads (raksha sutras) around the right wrist for men and the left wrist for women.

Do you ship sanctified holy water, like Ganga Jal?

Yes. For specific holy shrines, a hermetically sealed copper or premium plastic vial containing filtered holy water is securely bundled into your prasad kit where available.

Do you ship prasad and temple products internationally?

Yes. We support orders from around the globe. Prasad is cleared through custom export packaging regulations and shipped internationally to regions such as North America, Europe, Southeast Asia, and the Middle East.
