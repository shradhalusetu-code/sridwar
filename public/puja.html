How online puja booking works

Choose a puja and temple, share your name, Gotra, and date of birth, and an experienced local priest performs the ritual on your behalf at the scheduled time. You get a recording or photos of the puja along with prasad, so you stay connected to the ritual even from thousands of kilometers away.
