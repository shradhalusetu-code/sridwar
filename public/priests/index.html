Why priest profiles matter

When you can't travel to the temple yourself, knowing exactly who is performing your puja matters. Each priest profile lists their years of experience, the temples and deities they serve, their specific ritual expertise, and the languages they speak — so you can choose someone who understands your family's tradition and can communicate with you directly.

What our priests specialise in

  • Rudrabhishek, Laghu Rudra, and Mrityunjaya Homam at Jyotirlinga temples
  • Navagraha Shanti, Sade Sati remedies, and Durga Saptashati recitations
  • Festival-specific rituals such as Shravana Mela Jalabhishek
  • Guidance in Hindi, Maithili, Sanskrit, and other regional languages

What to look for before consulting a priest

Before booking, check a priest's years of experience, their puja expertise, the temple and city they currently serve, devotee ratings and reviews, and the languages they speak — this helps you choose someone who matches your family's tradition and can communicate with you clearly during a remote or online puja. Every completed puja includes photo or video proof sent back to the devotee, so you stay connected to the ritual even from thousands of kilometres away.
