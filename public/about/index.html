Why Sri Dwar exists

Millions of devotees live far from the temples their families have visited for generations — for work, for study, for a new life abroad. Sri Dwar exists to close that gap: experienced priests perform puja and seva on your behalf, live darshan brings the temple to your screen, and prasad ships to your door.

Who we serve

NRI and diaspora devotees, families staying connected across countries, and anyone who wants a trustworthy way to take part in temple tradition without being physically present. Support is available in English, Hindi, Bengali, and Odia.

Our mission

Sri Dwar wasn't built to replace a physical temple visit — it exists to bridge the distance for elderly parents, devotees who are unwell, and the global NRI community. Every Sankalpa registered on the platform is synced through a secure compliance system, with a major portion of the offering paid directly to the priests, temples, and local vendors who perform it.

Meet the founder

Sri Dwar was founded by Kunu Rana with a vision of preserving ancient faith through a fast, offline-friendly platform built for devotees in Europe, the Americas, and East Asia, as much as for those in India.
