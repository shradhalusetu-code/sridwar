Bridging Ancient Faith and Modern Technology

Sri Dwar was founded by Kunu Rana with a simple belief: physical distance from India shouldn't mean distance from your temple, your rituals, or your roots.

Why Sri Dwar exists

Millions of devotees live far from the temples their families have visited for generations — for work, for study, for a new life abroad. Sri Dwar exists to close that gap: verified priests perform puja and seva on your behalf, live darshan brings the temple to your screen, and prasad ships to your door.

Who we serve

NRI and diaspora devotees, families staying connected across countries, and anyone who wants a trustworthy way to take part in temple tradition without being physically present. Support is available in English, Hindi, Bengali, and Odia.
