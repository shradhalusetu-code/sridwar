Frequently asked questions

Is the food prepared for Annadanam completely pure vegetarian?

Yes. The food is cooked exclusively inside the official temple kitchens following rigorous cleanliness codes, with no onions or garlic, using pure cow ghee, organic seasonal grains, and traditional Vedic recipes.

How often should I sponsor a Gau Seva for optimal planetary peace?

According to Vedic astrology, serving holy cows on Wednesdays (Mercury), Thursdays (Jupiter), and Saturdays (Rahu/Ketu remedies) is considered auspicious. Sponsoring a monthly or annual Gau Seva is believed to support continuous karmic mitigation and home peace.

Will I receive an official donation receipt for Annadanam sponsorships?

Yes. Charitable sevas like Annadanam, when arranged through the pujaris we work with, generate an official donation receipt sent to your registered email within 7 working days, which may be eligible for tax exemption under applicable regional provisions. We recommend confirming eligibility with your tax advisor.

Can I dedicate a seva to someone else, like family living abroad?

Yes. During checkout you can dedicate the seva to a family member or friend and enter their name and intention. They can receive a personalized notification confirming the seva performed in their honor, along with photo proof, even if you're the one arranging it.

Do you offer bulk or corporate CSR seva packages?

Yes. We support bulk seva sponsorships for corporate CSR initiatives, community welfare drives, and group family functions, including consolidated billing and dedicated account management. Reach out to our Devotee Support team to set up a tailored organizational plan.
